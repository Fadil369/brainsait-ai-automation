# Legal Auditor Compliance Skills

This folder contains skills for legal compliance auditing and regulatory knowledge management for AI agents.

## Overview
The Legal Auditor Compliance skills enable AI agents to:
- Access and interpret precise laws and regulations
- Conduct automated compliance audits
- Generate standardized compliance reports
- Stay updated with regulatory changes

## Skill Categories

### 1. Regulatory Knowledge Base
- **Purpose**: Provide access to and interpretation of laws and regulations
- **Components**: EU AI Act, GDPR, ISO 27001 interpretations
- **Update Frequency**: Daily/Weekly based on legal updates

### 2. Auditing Scripts
- **Purpose**: Automated tools for privacy and compliance violation detection
- **Components**: PII detection, privacy policy compliance checking
- **Update Frequency**: Monthly/Weekly based on company policy

### 3. Compliance Reporting
- **Purpose**: Generate standardized compliance reports
- **Components**: SOC 2, ISO, internal audit report templates
- **Update Frequency**: Per audit cycle

## Implementation Notes
- All skills maintain focus on regulatory interpretation, not legal advice
- Skills reference legal materials and indicate need for lawyer review
- Arabic/English bilingual support for Middle Eastern markets
- HIPAA/GDPR compliance in authentication and data handling

## Integration Requirements
- Access to legal databases and regulatory updates
- Integration with company data systems (with proper permissions)
- Secure data handling with encryption during scanning
- Automatic deletion of temporary files
