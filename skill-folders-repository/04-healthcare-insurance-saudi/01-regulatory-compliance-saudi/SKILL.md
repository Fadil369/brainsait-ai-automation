# Saudi Healthcare Insurance Regulatory Compliance Skill

**Domain**: Compliance with Saudi Insurance Authority and Ministry of Health regulations  
**Application**: Exclusively for Saudi market  
**Complexity Level**: Advanced  
**Update Frequency**: Immediate with new regulations  

## Description
This skill enables the agent to understand and apply Saudi-specific healthcare insurance regulations, including the Cooperative Health Insurance system and Insurance Authority regulations.

## Core Components
1. **Saudi Regulations Library**:
   - Cooperative Health Insurance system
   - Healthcare insurance business regulation
   - Saudi Data Protection standards
   - Health Specialties Council requirements

2. **Local Interpretation Framework**:
   - Saudi Insurance Authority interpretations
   - Saudi Health Council fatwas
   - Ministry of Health decisions

## Procedural Instructions
1. Receive regulatory inquiry
2. Identify relevant regulatory authority (Insurance Authority, Health Council, Ministry)
3. Refer to precise regulatory text
4. Apply official interpretations
5. Provide answer with reference to Saudi decisions

## Attached Files
- `cchi-regulations-implementation.md`: Cooperative Health Insurance system implementation guide
- `sahi-regulatory-updates.md`: Automatic update system for Saudi decisions
- `saudi-healthcare-laws.md`: Saudi healthcare laws library

## Saudi Market Specifics
- Commitment to Arabic language in official documents
- Consideration of local practices and culture
- Understanding of Health Council approved price structure
