# Regulatory Knowledge Base Skill

**Domain**: Legal Compliance & Governance  
**Complexity Level**: Advanced  
**Estimated Time for Execution**: 2-3 minutes  
**Frequency**: Daily/Weekly based on legal updates  

## Description
This skill enables the agent to access and interpret laws and regulations accurately, relying on general inference rather than precise interpretation.

## Core Components
1. **Legislative Texts Library**: EU AI Act, GDPR, ISO 27001
2. **Legal Text Interpretation Rules**: Interpretation framework
3. **Automatic Legal Updates System**: Update mechanism

## Procedural Instructions
1. Receive inquiry about specific legal requirements
2. Search in the organization's legislative database
3. Apply standard interpretation rules
4. Provide answer with reference to legal materials

## Attached Knowledge Files
- `eu-ai-act-interpretation.md`: Guide to interpreting EU Artificial Intelligence Act articles
- `gdpr-compliance-framework.md`: Work framework for GDPR compliance

## Responsibility Limits
- Does not provide binding legal advice
- Indicates need for review by certified lawyer
- Focuses on regulatory interpretation, not legal opinion
